WELCOME TO OUR COMPANY

REMINE INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED is a formal recycling company dedicated to collecting and recycling metal waste, e-Waste, Li Batteries and scrap from rejected aluminum, utensil, containers, and other used metallic items, ensuring a cleaner and sustainable environment.

Our Services

E-Waste Recycling Services

We offer comprehensive e-waste recycling services to individuals and businesses. We handle electronic waste, from computers and smartphones to televisions, and repurpose all materials to reduce the environmental impact and promote sustainability.

Lithium Battery Recycling Services

Our lithium battery recycling services are designed to help individuals and businesses dispose of used or expired batteries in an environmentally friendly way. We collect all types of lithium batteries and ensure they are properly disposed.

Metal Recycling Services

Our metal recycling services help businesses and individuals by providing comprehensive recycling solutions for various metals, including copper, aluminum, and steel. We ensure that all metals are properly sorted and recycled, promoting sustainable resource management.
